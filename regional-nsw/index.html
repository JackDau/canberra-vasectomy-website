LIVING OUTSIDE OF CANBERRA?

Having trouble organising a vasectomy in regional New South Wales?

I perform between fifty to one hundred vasectomies annually on patients living anywhere between Parkes, Wagga and Bega.

If you travel more than one hour away, we can do the initial consultation via telehealth.

Monday Telehealth 3-5

Wednesday Telehealth 3-5

From that appointment, we can organise for you to have your procedure done with only one visit to Canberra.

Please watch the video on the home page before the initial consultation.

My staff will send an SMS the day before your initial telehealth consult to remind you to watch the video.
