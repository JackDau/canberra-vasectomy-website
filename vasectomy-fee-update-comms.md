# Vasectomy Service Update — March 2026

## What's Changing

### 1. New Fees (effective immediately)

| Item | Old Fee | New Fee | Medicare Rebate | Patient Out of Pocket |
|------|---------|---------|-----------------|----------------------|
| Initial Consultation | $117 | **$60** | $43.70 | $16.30 |
| Vasectomy Procedure | $911 | **$935** | $228 | $707 |
| Deposit | $200 | $200 (unchanged) | — | Subtracted from procedure fee |

### 2. Telehealth Now Available to ALL Patients

The initial consultation is now available **face-to-face or via telehealth** for all patients — not just regional NSW.

- Previously: telehealth was only offered to patients living more than 1 hour from Canberra
- Now: any patient can choose telehealth for their initial consult

**What to tell patients**: "Your initial consultation with Dr Deery can be done face-to-face at any of our three locations, or via telehealth — whichever suits you best."

### 3. Regional NSW Update

Dr Deery now performs over 100 vasectomies per year for men from regional NSW.
